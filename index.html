Turn campaign data into client-ready reports

Paste your Google Sheets link, pick a sheet, and ReportAI writes a polished performance report in seconds — then export it as a PowerPoint deck or Word document.

1
Paste your Google Sheets URL and analyze
Make sure your sheet is shared publicly (Anyone with link → Viewer). Click Analyze to fetch all tabs in your workbook and pick the one with campaign data.
2
Pick your sheet and check the preview
Select the correct tab from the list — you'll see a live 3-row preview so you can confirm it's the right data before generating.
3
Fill in report details and generate
Enter the client name, reporting period, and preferred tone. Hit Generate — Claude reads your data and writes a complete report in about 10 seconds.
4
Export as PowerPoint or Word
Copy the report, print it as PDF, or export slide-ready PowerPoint content and a formatted Word document — ready to send to your client.
